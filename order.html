How To Order

To order a patch, simply use the messaging box below to alert the creator that you would like to have a custom patch(s) made for either you or your team. You can specify the patch's image, colors, words to give the creator an idea of what you would like. Once the creator receives your request, he will reach out to you through the email address (that you will provide below) to discuss further details. During the production process, he will keep in contact with you and send you images of potential designs for the patch. The communication between you and the creator is important to making sure that the patch you order is to your liking and meets your expectations.
